🦎 Rain World Lizard Guide

Green Lizard

Common in most regions. Relatively docile unless provoked. Can be outmaneuvered by experienced slugcats.

Pink Lizard

Aggressive and territorial. Known for their speed and powerful bite. Best avoided unless necessary.

White Lizard

Extremely dangerous predators. Highly aggressive and difficult to escape from. Avoid at all costs.

Yellow Lizard

Quick and agile hunters that hunt in packs. Their coordinated attacks can overwhelm unprepared slugcats.

Salamander

Fire-aligned creatures that thrive in volcanic regions. Their body temperature can scorch unwary prey.

Caramel Lizard

Masters of camouflage in sandy environments. Their crystalline scales reflect light in mesmerizing patterns.

Spitter Lizard

Attacks with corrosive venom from a distance. Their toxic projectiles can melt through armor.

Black Lizard

Silent predators that hunt in darkness. Their red eyes are often the last thing their prey sees.

Evil Lizard

EVIL SCARY EVIL SCARY!!! Legends say that something special happens when you hover over it for too long... but since when was that true? :3

Survival Tips

  • Always observe lizard behavior before approaching
  • Use vertical spaces to your advantage
  • Carry a spear for self-defense
  • Learn each lizard's territory and patrol patterns
  • When in doubt, hide in a shelter or pipe
