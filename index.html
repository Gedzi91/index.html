How to Write Custom Lines

To write custom lines for Hoops and Yoyo, follow these formats:

For Hoops:
Start the line with "Hoops:" followed by what you want Hoops to say
Example: Hoops: Hey Yoyo, want to go on an adventure?

For Yoyo:
Start the line with "Yoyo:" followed by what you want Yoyo to say
Example: Yoyo: That sounds awesome, let's do it!

Each line will automatically be colored and animated according to who's speaking!
