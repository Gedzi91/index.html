Backstory

Once a simple tree-dwelling rodent, Nutkin's life changed forever when he discovered an abandoned miniature firearm near his oak. Fascinated by its power, he taught himself to use it, becoming an unlikely sharpshooter. Now, Nutkin roams the forest, protecting his furry friends from predators and occasionally causing mischief for unsuspecting hikers.
