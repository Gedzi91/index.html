Sonic Characters: Twisted Fates

WARNING: This content contains extremely disturbing themes and graphic descriptions. Viewer discretion strongly advised.

The Graced Messenger (Sonic)

The Graced Messenger, once Sonic, now a nightmarish entity. Dark blue fur matted with blood, head crowned with razor-sharp spikes. Left eye socket gapes empty, oozing malevolence. Pulsating red spot mars his face. Remaining eye burns with hellish intent. Red shoes leave bloody footprints, marking his path of destruction.

Tails

Tails remains physically unchanged, a cruel twist of fate. Orange fur appears duller, eyes haunted by witnessed horrors. Twin tails often wrap protectively around him. Posture tense, once-cheerful demeanor replaced by constant vigilance and barely contained terror.

Knuckles

Knuckles, once proud guardian, now a grotesque sight. Left eye violently torn out, leaving bloody, weeping socket. Remaining eye wild with pain and paranoia. Blood constantly drips, staining red fur darker. Powerful hands twitch with barely contained violence.

Amy

Amy, once cheerful, now a horrific sight. Limbs brutally torn off, leaving bloody stumps. Torso split open, exposing rearranged organs. Face frozen in terror, mouth crudely stitched shut. Pink fur matted with blood and gore. Motionless, a silent testament to unimaginable cruelty.

Dr. Eggman

Dr. Eggman, once formidable, now a mangled mess. Corpulent form torn open, organs spilling out grotesquely. Eyes gouged out, leaving dark, bloody sockets. Signature mustache matted with gore. Legs violently ripped off, leaving jagged stumps. A gruesome reminder of the Graced Messenger's wrath.

Shadow

Shadow's fate unknown. Last seen May 4, 2014. Only remaining image: a tattered missing poster. Black fur with red streaks, resembling a darker Sonic. His true fate remains a haunting mystery in this nightmarish world.

Metal Sonic (Withered)

Metal Sonic, once a fearsome machine, now a rusted, broken shell. Metallic blue body corroded and dented. One eye shattered, the other flickering weakly. Limbs bent at unnatural angles, sparks occasionally flying from exposed wiring. A pitiful remnant of former robotic glory, left to decay in this twisted world.
