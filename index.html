SCP-914: The Clockworks

WARNING: Use of SCP-914 may result in unexpected and potentially dangerous outcomes. Proceed with caution.

SCP-914 Wiki

Settings:

  • Rough: Causes severe degradation or simplification of the input.
  • Coarse: Degrades or simplifies the input, but less severely than Rough.
  • 1:1: Produces an output similar to the input, but with minor alterations.
  • Fine: Refines or improves the input in various ways.
  • Very Fine: Significantly alters the input, often producing anomalous or unexpected results.
  • Split: Attempts to divide the input into multiple related components or objects.
  • Random: Produces highly unpredictable and chaotic alterations to the input, ranging from subtle changes to complete transformations.

Buttons:

  • Process Item: Submits the input text to SCP-914 for processing using the selected setting.
  • Reuse Last Output: Replaces the input text with the most recent output, allowing for iterative processing.
