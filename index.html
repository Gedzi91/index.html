Strikhy The Crew-Nacked Parakeet Event

Event Name: Strikhy The Crew-Nacked Parakeet Date: April 2009 - December 2013 Collaboration: Logitech × Disney Location: Club Penguin Island Free Item: Classic Arcade Strikhy T-Shirt Character Type: Family-Friendly Game Player Original Debut: 1984 (Arcade Game)

Event Description

The Strikhy The Crew-Nacked Parakeet Event was a special crossover event in Club Penguin, featuring a collaboration between Logitech and Disney. This long-running event, which lasted from April 2009 to December 2013, celebrated Strikhy's legacy from the original 1984 arcade game, bringing both nostalgic and new gaming experiences to Club Penguin Island until several years before the platform's eventual shutdown in 2017. The event was particularly special because Strikhy's character design resonated with Club Penguin's art style, making the parakeet feel like a natural addition to the penguin community.

Features

  • Exclusive Strikhy-themed items and accessories for penguins
  • Meet-and-greet sessions with Strikhy at various locations around the island
  • New "Strikhy's Flight Challenge" mini-game for Club Penguin Game Day on the Wii
  • Classic 1984 Strikhy arcade cabinet available in the Dance Club
  • Special screenings of the 2001 Strikhy cartoon series at the Coffee Shop and Pizza Parlor

Classic Strikhy Arcade (1984)

The original arcade game features Strikhy solving increasingly challenging mazes while racing against time. Players guide Strikhy through complex labyrinths, discovering hidden paths and collecting power-ups along the way. Now available to play in the Dance Club!

Strikhy Cartoon Series (2001)

Watch episodes of the beloved cartoon series "Strikhy's Gaming Adventures" at the Coffee Shop and Pizza Parlor. Showing daily from 10 AM to 8 PM penguin time.

Collectible Items

  • Classic Arcade Strikhy T-Shirt (Complete 10 mazes)
  • Cartoon Strikhy Plush Backpack (Watch 3 episodes)
  • Strikhy's Game Day Trophy (Complete Wii mini-game)
  • Retro Strikhy Pin (1984 design)

Trivia

  • The original 1984 Strikhy arcade game was created by legendary game designer Ken Williams
  • The 2001 cartoon series ran for 3 seasons with 65 episodes total
  • This event marks the first time the original arcade game has been officially re-released
  • The new Wii mini-game features updated graphics while maintaining the classic maze-solving gameplay style
  • Strikhy's design was chosen for this collaboration due to its similarity to Club Penguin's character style
