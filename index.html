About the Demo

This interactive demonstration showcases DreamTech AI's cutting-edge holographic particle simulation technology. By manipulating millions of virtual particles in real-time, we can create stunning visual effects that mimic the behavior of light in holographic displays.

Use the controls to interact with the particles, change their patterns, and explore different shapes. The fullscreen mode offers an immersive experience, allowing you to fully appreciate the intricacy and beauty of the particle interactions.

  • Real-time particle simulation with up to 1 million particles
  • Multiple interaction patterns inspired by quantum mechanics
  • Dynamic shape morphing capabilities
  • Responsive design for various screen sizes
  • Optimized performance using WebGL and GPU acceleration
