How to Play Tile Busters

  • Click Start to begin the game
  • You have 20 seconds to score as many points as possible
  • Click on neon-colored tiles to score points:
    • Regular neon tiles = 1 point
    • BONUS tiles start at 2 points and get +1 point more valuable each time you click one
    • JACKPOT tiles = DOUBLE your current score!
    • You have 2 chances to click the JACKPOT tile
    • If you miss it twice, it turns into a regular neon tile
  • Special Tiles:
    • JACKPOT tiles appear rarely (1 in 75 chance)
    • They have a rainbow gradient effect
    • Clicking a JACKPOT tile doubles your entire score!
  • Watch out for black tiles!
    • They appear every 7 neon tiles you tap
    • Clicking a black tile reduces your score by 5 points
    • Be careful! Some black tiles are extra unlucky (1 in 40 chance)
    • Unlucky black tiles will cut your score in HALF!
    • Tap any neon tile to remove the black tile
  • Bonus Frenzies:
    • At 3 seconds remaining, there's a chance for a bonus frenzy!
    • During a frenzy, 2 tiles will always be bonus tiles
    • The other 2 tiles become black tiles
    • Tiles randomly change positions during the frenzy
    • Black tiles reduce score by 5% during frenzies
  • Try to beat your high score!

Game Summary

Tile Busters is the perfect game to test your speed, skill, and processing speed. In this fast-paced game, quickly realizing which tiles are which is crucial for getting a big highscore in this game. Whether you're looking for a game to challenge yourself, play with a friend, or just play for fun, this game is perfect for you!
