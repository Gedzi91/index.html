Blackbearry

A mysterious creature that roams the snowy landscape. Known for its peculiar walking pattern and aggressive pursuit of intruders.

  • Behavior: Patrols its territory and chases nearby players
  • Detection Range: 10 units
  • Movement: Wanders when idle, actively pursues when detecting players
  • Special Traits: Can track player movement and adjust its path accordingly
Parasite

A sinister entity that appears to have taken over what was once an innocent teddy bear. Known for its aggressive nature and rapid movements.

  • Behavior: Aggressively hunts players with high speed
  • Detection Range: 10 units
  • Movement: Fast-paced chase with fluid animation
  • Special Traits: Deals increased damage, moves faster than other enemies
Asteroid

A mysterious stone construct that lumbers through the environment. Though slower than other enemies, it is more durable and hits harder.

  • Behavior: Slow but steady pursuit of targets
  • Detection Range: 12 units
  • Movement: Slow but persistent
  • Special Traits: Higher health pool, increased damage, larger detection range
Space Invader

A mysterious alien entity that appears during invasion events. Armed with advanced technology and superior mobility.

  • Behavior: Aggressive pursuit with tactical movements
  • Detection Range: 15 units
  • Movement: Fast and agile
  • Special Traits: Appears during alien invasion events, enhanced detection range
